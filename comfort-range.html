AcoustiX Comfort Range

Designed for warmth, colour and texture — delivering acoustic comfort with personality.

Comfort Range

The AcoustiX Comfort Range combines high acoustic performance with a curated palette of colours and textures. Made with up to 75% post-consumer recycled PET, the Comfort Range is ideal for offices, hospitality and residential spaces seeking both sound control and aesthetic warmth.

Use our panels as wall features, suspended ceiling rafts, desk screens and freestanding dividers — all available in multiple thicknesses and bespoke sizes.

Key Features

Acoustic Performance

NRC up to 0.9 depending on mounting — effectively reduces reverberation and improves speech clarity.

Warmth & Texture

Soft, tactile surface with visual depth — adds warmth to interiors while absorbing sound.

Colour Variety

20+ designer tones across Warm, Cool and Neutral palettes — easily coordinate with interiors.

Sustainable

Made from up to 75% post-consumer PET — SCS verified and OEKO-TEX compliant.

Acoustic Comfort Solutions

Wall Panels

Acoustic wall panels designed to absorb sound and reduce echo in open environments.
