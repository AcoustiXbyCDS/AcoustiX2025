Drawer Systems

Stay organized and clutter-free with durable storage solutions designed for modern workspaces.

Why Drawer Systems?

Our desk drawers are designed to help you stay organized and focused. Whether it’s office supplies, paperwork, or personal items, CDS Plastics has a range of solutions to suit every workspace.

Our Range

TDL – That Desk Locker

Sturdy desk drawer with telescopic runners, attachment brackets, and a multi-user combination lock. 577mm wide × 390mm deep × 50mm high. Available in Black.

Bizz Drawer

Desk organizer with compartmentalized sections. Fits 364mm/380mm drawers with 400mm runner. Injection-moulded, supports central locking. Available in Black.

BV Drawer

High-quality storage with 450mm runner for 364mm carcass. Made from recycled ABS plastic with built-in central locking. Available in Black.

Varta Drawer

Robust storage with 570mm runner for 380mm carcass. Made from recycled ABS plastic with slide adapters. Available in Black.

Pencil Tray

Compact storage for small supplies. 4 compartments. Fits BV and Varta drawers. Available in small and medium sizes, Black.

Filing Frame

Compact filing for A4 and foolscap hanging files. Lightweight and durable. Use with fascia brackets for added support. Available in Black.
