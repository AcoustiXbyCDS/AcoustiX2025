Cable Management

Keeping a work environment and desk neat and tidy without cables getting tangled and destroyed is a problem for many individuals and companies. CDS has a range of simple and elegant cable management solutions to suit any environment.

PELICAN

The Pelican is the newest product launched by CDS Plastics. It allows you to customise your cabling solution easily.

SNAIK

LINKIT VERTICAL & HORIZONTAL

A great solution to cable mess. Linkit is a universal cable carrier designed for easy installation and clean cable organisation with attractive colour options.

CABLE HIGHWAY

Keeps plugs off the ground and hidden under the desk while maximising leg room without disruption.

SUPER SPRING

Designed for modern sit/stand desks, safely routing cables from desktop to floor while adjusting in height.

CABLE OUTLET (CIRCULAR & SQUARE)

An aesthetic desk finish allowing cables to pass neatly through surfaces while preventing damage and improving visual appeal.
