Why Cable Management?

Tidy cables make for safer, more productive, and more professional environments. Our cable management range is designed to hide clutter, improve safety, and enhance the overall look of your workspace.

Cable Basket

Strong under-desk basket for neatly routing power and data cables. Provides ventilation and easy access for maintenance.

Cable Spine

Flexible vertical cable spine that guides cables from desk to floor, ideal for sit-stand desks and dynamic office layouts.

Cable Tray

Wide, durable tray for managing power blocks and adapters under your desk. Keeps equipment safe and accessible.

Desk Grommets

Discreet pass-throughs for routing desktop cables. Available in multiple finishes to match your workspace design.

The Benefits

Safety

Reduce tripping hazards and prevent cable damage.

Neatness

Create a clean, professional workspace appearance.

Flexibility

Adaptable to modern desks and evolving office needs.
