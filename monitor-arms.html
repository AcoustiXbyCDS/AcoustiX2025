Agilis Monitor Arms

The Agilis Computer Monitor Arm is a multi-directional ergonomic monitor support system designed to support single or multiple monitors. Since 2002, Agilis has been proudly manufactured in South Africa, helping reduce back and neck strain through ergonomic positioning.

Agilis is compatible with monitors using a VESA mounting pattern and can be installed by clamping or bolting onto desks. Flexible positioning and wall-mount options help maximise workspace efficiency.

Manufactured using durable virgin materials, Agilis follows environmentally responsible production processes, ensuring recyclable components and long-lasting performance.
