AcoustiX PET Panels

AcoustiX PET is a sustainable, eco-friendly solution made from 50% recycled polyester fibre. It offers excellent acoustic and insulation properties, is 100% recyclable and has a minimum lifespan of 10 years.

About AcoustiX

AcoustiX PET Panels by CDS Plastics are designed to reduce reverberation and improve acoustic quality. Manufactured using recycled PET, they combine performance, durability and sustainability — lightweight, fire-tested and suitable for commercial and residential fit-outs.

Technical Details

Thickness

9mm, 12mm, 24mm

Standard Sheet Size

2440 × 1220 × 9mm (standard). Teal board: 2800 × 1200 × 9mm.

Acoustic Rating

NRC up to 0.9 depending on thickness & mounting.
