AcoustiX PET Panels

Engineered for acoustic comfort, sustainability and elegant design.

About AcoustiX

AcoustiX is a decorative and functional acoustic panel system designed to reduce reverberation and enhance sound quality in modern spaces. Made from up to 75% post-consumer recycled PET plastic, the panels are lightweight, durable, and fire-safe, offering an eco-friendly solution for interiors that demand both performance and style.

Features & Benefits

High Acoustic Absorption

Noise Reduction Coefficient (NRC) up to 0.9 depending on installation method.

Recycled & Sustainable

Made from 75% post-consumer PET bottles with SCS certification.

Fire Rated

Complies with EN13501-1: B-s1,d0 fire safety standards.

Design Flexibility

Available in multiple colours, shapes, and custom sizes.

Locally Manufactured

Proudly made in South Africa using sustainable processes.

Eco-Friendly

Fully recyclable at end-of-life for a circular economy approach.

Technical Details

Thickness

9mm, 12mm, and 24mm options available.

Sheet Size

1220mm x 2440mm standard size; custom dimensions available on request.

Weight

1.2–2.4 kg/m² depending on thickness.

Composition

Up to 75% post-consumer recycled PET.

Fire Rating

EN13501-1: B-s1,d0 (Tested on 9mm).

Acoustic Rating
