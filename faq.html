PET Panels – FAQ
How can you install PET panels?
PET Felt panels have a different map on the front as to the back. That’s because the fibres are differently oriented. Always lay out the panels before you mount them. To install the panels, we recommend using a polymer adhesive sealant, such as High Tack. The advantage of using this adhesive is that it does not immediately get hard, so you can still adjust while mounting. The kit is not included with the panels, but it can be found at any construction materials store. PET Felt panels can also be mounted with a tackler and headle
How do I clean PET felt?
PET Felt is easy to clean and maintain. Most stains can be removed with plenty of water. Let the water drip through and blot the stain with a dry cloth. Never rub the PET Felt, as it can damage the material. Stubborn stains can be removed with special cleaning agents such as clean green
How resistant are PET Felt products to impact and wear?
Since PET Felt is made of recycled plastic bottles, it can withstand a lot. PET Felt is also impact resistant. However, the material should still be handled with care, so do not place it somewhere humid or dirty, as this will make it wear out quickly
What is NRC?
NRC stands for Noise Reduction Coefficient. It is determined by measurements on the frequencies of 250 Hz, 500 Hz, 1000 Hz and 2000 Hz. An NRC value of 0 corresponds to a complete reflection of sound. At the other end of the scale, we speak of complete absorption of the sound, in this case, the NRC value is 1
